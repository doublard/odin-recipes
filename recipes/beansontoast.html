How To Make Beans on Toast

Description

Are you a fan of Beans? If so, you'll absolutely love this recipe that can serve up to 1 person!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Empty beans into a bowl
  2. Put Bowl in microwave for 2 minutes (I think. idk never made them)
  3. Put bread in toaster until brown and toast like
  4. Put beans on toast
  5. mmmm beans
