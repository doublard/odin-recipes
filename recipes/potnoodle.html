How To Make A Pot Noodle

Description

Are you a fan of Pot Noodle? If so, you'll absolutely love this recipe that can serve up to 1 person!

Ingredients

  • Pot Noodle
  • Kettle
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Fork

Steps

  1. Open the pot noodle and remove sachet
  2. Add water to kettle and boil it
  3. After kettle has boiled fill the pot noodle to the line with water
  4. Allow to sit for 3 minutes
  5. Add contents of the sachet in the pot noodle
  6. Empty salt shaker into pot noodle
  7. Add the fork and stir until ready to eat
  8. Throw it in the trash and get some real food
